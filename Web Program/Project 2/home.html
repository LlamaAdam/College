Information

Location of the zoo

The zoo is located in downtown near the grocery store.

Contact information

Our contact phone number is 816-876-5309.
Our email address is: DowntownZoo@gmail.com

Hours of operation

The Zoo operates from 9:00 A.M. to 7:00 P.M. Monday thru Friday. On the weekends The Zoo operates from 9:00 A.M. to 5:30 P.M.

Dates of operation

The zoo operates all year but it does contain seasonal exhibits. The exhibits that are scheduled can be found in the Calendar.

Description of the zoo

The zoo contains a various collection of animals. The exhibits are broken into 3 different sections Petting, Sahara, and previously extinct animals.

Prices

Adults: $25.00, Children: $10.00
Senior: $13.00, Student: $13.00
